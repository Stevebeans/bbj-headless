### Key Design Decisions
- Resend as email provider (replaces MailPoet at $21.25/mo)
- Double opt-in for anonymous subscribers, auto-confirm for logged-in users
- Async post notification via WP Cron (non-blocking publish)
- HMAC-SHA256 unsubscribe tokens
- Svix webhook signature verification
- Settings stored in `bbjd_email_settings` WP option
- Email tables: `wp_bbj_email_subscribers`, `wp_bbj_email_lists`, `wp_bbj_email_list_subscribers`, `wp_bbj_email_sends`
