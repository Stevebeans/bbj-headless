# Staging Update - Feb 7, 2026

Hey everyone! Welcome to the new BBJ staging site. This is a complete rebuild of BigBrotherJunkies from the ground up — faster, cleaner, and packed with new features. Here's what's new and what to poke around at:

---

## What's New

- **Completely redesigned site** — The whole thing has been rebuilt for speed. Pages load way faster, images are optimized, and everything just feels snappier.

- **New login & registration** — Revamped login page with Google sign-in support. If you've been having logout issues on the main site, this should be a huge improvement. You can also link your Google account to your existing BBJ account.

- **Comment system overhaul** — Comments got a major glow-up:
  - User ranks based on your comment activity
  - Online status indicators (see who's active)
  - Quick reply to other comments
  - Upvote/downvote reactions
  - Better threading so conversations are easier to follow

- **Custom avatars** — You can now upload your own profile picture! Head to your Settings page to set it up.

- **Notification bell** — You'll see a bell icon in the header now. It'll light up when someone replies to your comment, mentions you, or when we post site announcements. No more missing conversations.

- **Site announcements** — We can now push updates directly to your notification bell. You'll see a preview in the dropdown and can tap through to read the full thing.

- **Player directory & profiles** — Browse all BB players with filters and sorting. Each player has their own profile page with stats, seasons, and castmates.

- **Season profiles** — Each season has its own page with player rosters and details.

- **User settings page** — Manage your profile, avatar, linked accounts, and preferences all in one place.

- **Back-to-top button** — Little quality of life thing — a button pops up when you scroll down so you can zip back to the top.

- **Dark mode** — Toggle between light and dark themes from the settings bar.

- **Contact form** — If you're logged in, it auto-fills your name and email. Easy.

---

## What to Test

We'd love your help breaking things! Here's what to look at:

- **Log in and out a few times** — Does it stick? Try both email/password and Google sign-in if you have it linked.
- **Post some comments** — Reply to people, try the upvote/downvote, see if threading makes sense.
- **Check your notifications** — Comment on something, have someone reply, see if the bell picks it up.
- **Upload an avatar** — Go to Settings and try uploading a profile pic.
- **Browse player pages** — Search, filter, click around the player profiles.
- **Try it on your phone** — The whole site should work great on mobile. Let us know if anything looks off.
- **Dark mode** — Toggle it and see if anything looks weird or unreadable.

---

## Found a Bug?

There's a **bug report button** (little icon in the bottom-right corner of the screen). If you're logged in, just tap it, describe what happened, and it sends it straight to us. Use it for anything — broken layouts, weird behavior, things that don't look right. Screenshots help too!

---

Thanks for helping us test! This is all leading up to the BB28 launch and your feedback makes a huge difference.
